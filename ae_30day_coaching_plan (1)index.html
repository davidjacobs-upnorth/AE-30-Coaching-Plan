Day 30 review outcomes
Plan complete
Full quota met — $8,000 MRR, 36 deals, ≥90% hardware attach, 48 demos. Coaching plan closed. AE returns to standard performance management.
Escalate to performance plan
If targets are not met, manager determines whether to extend coaching, restructure support, or escalate to a formal 30-day performance improvement plan.
Escalation to a performance plan — considerations
If the Day 30 review reveals sustained underperformance, a formal performance improvement plan may be initiated. The following factors inform that decision:
  • MRR closed below $6,400 (80% of quota) at end of Day 30
  • Fewer than 29 deals closed (80% of the 36-deal target)
  • Hardware attach rate below 85% for the month
  • Fewer than 38 demos completed across the 30-day period
  • Show rate consistently below 65% despite coaching interventions
  • Repeated failure to follow daily activity expectations or check-in cadence
  • No measurable improvement in identified skill gaps across the 4 weeks
