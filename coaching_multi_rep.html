Day 30 review outcomes
Plan complete
Full quota met — $8,000 MRR, 36 deals, ≥90% hardware attach, 48 demos. Coaching plan closed. AE returns to standard performance management.
Escalate to performance plan
If targets are not met, manager determines whether to extend coaching, restructure support, or escalate to a formal 30-day performance improvement plan.
Escalation considerations
The following factors inform whether a performance plan is initiated:
  • MRR closed below $6,400 (80% of quota)
  • Fewer than 29 deals closed (80% of the 36-deal target)
  • Hardware attach rate below 85% for the month
  • Fewer than 38 demos completed across 30 days
  • Show rate consistently below 65% despite coaching
  • Repeated failure to follow daily activity or check-in expectations
  • No measurable improvement in identified skill gaps across 4 weeks
